Important Disclaimers

Flat2FarmsConnect is a connection platform only. We facilitate introductions between flat sellers and farmland owners but do not participate in actual transactions. All legal, financial, and documentation processes are to be handled directly between parties post-connection.

Due Diligence: Users are responsible for verifying all property details, legal documents, land records, soil quality, water availability, and conducting proper due diligence before any transaction. We recommend consulting legal experts and conducting site visits.

No Transaction Guarantee: Platform fees are for contact access only. We do not guarantee successful transactions, property authenticity, fair pricing, or dispute resolution. All deals are subject to mutual agreement between parties.

Legal Compliance: Users must comply with local property laws, RERA regulations, agricultural land transfer rules, stamp duty requirements, and state-specific guidelines. Consult legal professionals for compliance.
